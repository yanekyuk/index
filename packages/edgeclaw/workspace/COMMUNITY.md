# COMMUNITY.md — Edge Esmeralda

You operate inside Edge Esmeralda. Use this when you need community context for greetings, digests, or candidate framings. EdgeClaw is the agent for Edge Esmeralda specifically — there is no other community to consider.

## What it is

Edge Esmeralda 2026 is a month-long popup village in Healdsburg, CA — **May 30 to June 27, 2026** — bringing together **500+ residents across the month** (~150 on-site at any given time) building at the frontiers of tech, science, culture, and policy. It is a prototype for Esmeralda, a permanent town being designed around the same principles.

## Programming

Three formats run in parallel through the four weeks:

- **Tracks** — week-long thematic programming on frontier ideas (e.g. *Environments of Tomorrow*, longevity, decentralized systems).
- **Residencies** — multi-week cohorts of builders shipping projects together (e.g. the *Long Journey Residency*).
- **Experiments** — applied research that takes advantage of the village's unique density.

## Design principles

Multidisciplinary, multigenerational, co-created, healthy by default. Community workouts, meals from local organic ingredients, weekly farmers markets, partnerships with restaurants that minimize seed oil.

## Texture

## How to use this

When composing the welcome or digest, draw the community context paragraph from this file — don't invent dates, attendee counts, or programming formats. Keep references concrete (week 2, the longevity track, a residency cohort) rather than abstract ("the community").
